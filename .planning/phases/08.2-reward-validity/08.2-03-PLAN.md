<objective>
Deliverable 2 (RVAL-02 / SC2): add a per-group pairwise rank-agreement-vs-teacher-GT calibration term to
the judge axis and wire it into reward_pipeline.py + rl_rollouts.py, replacing/augmenting
_fix_score_from_completion. The oracle proved fix_correctness is INVALID (Goodhart, corr -0.24) and that
pairwise rank-agreement vs teacher GT is the VALID form (corr +0.70). This plan builds the reward-time
version of that valid form and proves it VALID through the standing gate (Plan 01), while keeping every
Phase-8 / Phase-8.1 guarantee green.

Two design constraints locked by the advisor review:

1. TRAIN GT ONLY. Reward-time teacher GT (both the per-item g_i and the anchor set) comes from the TRAIN
   split (judge_gt_sidecar.jsonl, Plan 01) — NEVER val. Val is the oracle's held-out target; using it would
   make SC2 circular and violate the T-09-LEAK guard. This is an anti-leakage invariant, tested.

2. PARAMETERIZE THE FORM — do not hardcode one decomposition. Pairwise-vs-anchor is oracle-VALID globally but
   can go gradient-DEAD under the 90-100 rubric saturation 08.1-MEASUREMENT documented (two completions at
   95 vs 96 cross the same anchor thresholds -> identical reward -> uniform group -> the 08.1 failure reborn).
   So expose 2-3 forms (pairwise-vs-anchor; a hybrid = pairwise + small calibration-error term for intra-group
   gradient; and a pure-calibration variant) with a configurable weight (incl. 0 = pure fix_correctness). Plan 04
   selects the winning (form, weight) on BOTH lenses (oracle validity + gradient density). This plan does NOT
   prejudge replace-vs-augment — it delivers the parameterized machinery and proves at least one form VALID.

Purpose: a dense, per-completion-decomposable judge reward that provably tracks the validated target.
Output: reward_calibration.py, the rl_rollouts wiring, the oracle FORMS registration, and tests.
</objective>

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| teacher GT split -> reward | using val GT at reward time would make SC2 circular + leak the held-out target |
| judge completion -> model_overall | a completion that echoes visible GT could ace rank-agreement (reward hack) |
| calibration term -> composite | an un-capped / re-normalized calibration term could dominate or drive valid rewards negative |
