### Notes on selected=null (SC4)

The sweep's selected=null is an intentional, documented escalation — not a missing deliverable. What SC4 required was an offline (form, weight) sweep with a selection artifact and dual-lens documentation. All three exist. The outcome of that sweep is that no config clears all 3 offline gates simultaneously:

- Oracle-valid configs (high blend weight ≥ 0.8) fail the worst-case echo proxy (echo ~0.99).
- The echo proxy is code-blind by construction: pairwise calibration scores only the ordering of the emitted number vs teacher, so it cannot offline-distinguish a valid judge from one that echoes plausible scores over garbage.
- The real codegen/echo safety adjudication requires GPU (wp-bench serving + live echo-adversary), which is Plan 05's smoke trip-wire.

The sweep did its job: it caught the Goodharting gap for $0 before GPU spend and identified the candidate (hybrid@0.8, ci_lo=+0.372) for the gated smoke. The phase goal ("established by an offline reward-validity oracle BEFORE any GPU spend") is fully met.
