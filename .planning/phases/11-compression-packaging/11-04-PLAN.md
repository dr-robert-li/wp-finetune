<objective>
Build the training-free k-sweep: an inference-time expert-masking path that drops the coldest
routed experts per layer at budgets k in {13,32,64} while always retaining protected + hot experts,
then measure wp-bench (gen) and judge ensemble rho (3-seed median) at each k plus the full unmasked
baseline. This is the SIEVE-04 deliverable — "adapter checkpoint per k" reinterpreted as
"expert-mask + eval record per k" under the locked training-free scope.

Purpose: Find how aggressively experts can be dropped before gen/judge quality degrades, feeding the
plan-11-05 TOST optimal-k decision and the Phase-13 AIMER prune-set.
Output: sieve_expert_mask_inference.py + output/sieve/k_sweep_results.json.
</objective>
